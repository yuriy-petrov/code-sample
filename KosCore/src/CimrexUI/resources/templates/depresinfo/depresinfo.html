General Remark

Depressurisation is only possible from the following steps:
Step 0: Standby Nitrogen Purge
Step 9: Standby Hydrogen Purge
Step 30: Standby Hydrogen Product

This action will lead the system back to step 0 from where only the Nitrogen-Purge cycle can be selected.

The depressurisation will be stopped whe the system pressure is 15 kPa or lower
